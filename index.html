our purpose

The purpose is to nurture the root of the plant!

At The Happiness Movement, our purpose is to cultivate personal growth and well-being by nurturing the roots of individuals, just like tending to the core of a plant.

We believe that true transformation and holistic happiness can only be achieved by nourishing the essence of who we are.

Just as cleaning the leaves of a plant alone cannot sustain its growth, we understand that focusing solely on external circumstances or superficial aspects of life is not enough to foster lasting fulfilment.

Our purpose is to guide individuals in discovering and nurturing the core of their being—their beliefs, values, emotions, and aspirations—so that they can effectively care for all spheres of their lives.

our vision

At The Happiness Movement, our vision is to redefine the way society approaches counseling.

We strive to create a world where seeking emotional support and prioritizing our inner well-being is not only normalized but celebrated.

our mission

Our mission is to guide individuals on a transformative journey that goes beyond the surface level and delves deep into their true emotions, empowering them to cultivate genuine happiness from within.

We envision a future where people are encouraged to embrace their emotional experiences and engage in open conversations about their mental well-being.

Through our counseling services, we aim to break the stigma surrounding seeking help and foster an environment of understanding, compassion, and growth.
Meet the founder

Introducing Dinesh Manchanda, a multifaceted individual whose journey has been nothing short of remarkable. It all began with his graduation in design from Symbiosis International University, where he honed his creative prowess and laid the foundation for an illustrious career collaborating with industry icons like Manish Malhotra and contributing to the success of the renowned brand, Raymond.

In 2017, a profound spiritual awakening set Dinesh on a transformative course, igniting his passion for inner exploration and growth. After this wonderful spiritual experience, Dinesh Manchanda furthered his spiritual journey by learning Raja Yoga meditation from the “Brahma Kumaris” organisation. Today, he stands not only as a meditation teacher but also dedicates himself to spiritual service with the organisation.

Driven by a newfound interest in counseling, Dinesh delved into the study of human psychology and well-being. His quest for knowledge led him to pursue post-graduation in Counseling and Hypnotherapy from Anna Malai University. Armed with a wealth of insight and understanding, he emerged as a certified counselor and therapist, ready to empower lives. As the founder and core counselor of The Happiness Moment, Dinesh aims to reintroduce and redefine counseling in our society. He envisions counseling as something very normal and casual, treating it with the utmost comfort and care. In the realm of The Happiness Moment, Dinesh Manchanda invites you to explore the boundless possibilities of personal growth and inner fulfilment. Welcome to a world where happiness knows no boundaries and where every step you take is accompanied by compassion and support.
